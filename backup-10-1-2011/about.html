About Dmitry

Hi, my name is Dmitry and I have over 10 years of experience in web, flash and print design as well as web development. I am based in Brooklyn, NY. In 1998, I received a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphics Design from the Fashion Institute of Technology, New York. I specialize in designing beautiful and easy to use websites. I also focus on print design and corporate identity.

Throughout my experiences, I have developed solid knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash and QuarkXpress as well as web development languages including HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, ActionScript and AJAX. I am also experienced working with content management systems, WordPress, blogs and social media websites. Having a background in both design and development allows me to easily work alongside developers on large scale projects.

I have an interest in everything design related including typography, interfaces, UX, multimedia and motion. Throughout my education, I have always excelled in artistic endeavors. This led me to head down the creative route in school by choosing art related subjects.

I approach all projects with enthusiasm and effectiveness. Projects ideas are transformed into beautiful and usable web and interactive design experiences. If you would like me to take on your design project, multimedia application or animation sequence, I would love to hear from you!
