Olymp Medical Logo

Client

Olymp Medical

Industry

Health Care

Project Type

Logo Creation, Branding and Identity, Print

Project Summary

New medical office In Brooklyn, New York needed an identity. The goal was to create more of an emblem than a regular logo. Various medical themes had to be incorporated into it as well as some ancient greek elements. Working with one of the doctors who advised me about the industry, I created a composition of various symbols that were stylized to work in harmony with one another.

Tools used

Pen & paper, Adobe Illustrator

Project Date

2006
